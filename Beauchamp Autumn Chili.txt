Autumn Chili
-2 tablespoons of Brown Sugar
-3 tablespoons of Chili powder
-1 16 oz can of diced roasted tomatoes
-1/2 red onion
-1 small white onion
-3 cloves garlic
-1 lb ground beef or bison
-28 oz can of black beans
-28 oz can of bush's country style baked beans
-2 teaspoons of smoked paprkia
-1 teaspoon onion powder
-1 teaspoon garlic powder
-1 teaspoon cumin
-1 teaspoon Ancho Chili Powder
-1.5 teaspoon of smoked smalt
-1/2 teaspoon white pepper
-1/2 teaspoon black pepper
-1 teaspoon nutmeg
-1/2 teaspoon cinammon
-1 teaspoon allspice
-Dash of Cloves
-1 16 oz can of Fiesta corn
-1 jar of roasted red and yellow peppers
-1 16 oz bottle of Pumpkick pumpkin beer

Dice Onions, Mince Garlic, Dice Peppers
Brown Beef (add Onion and Garlic to pan)
Combine all ingredients in crockpot
Cook for 6-8 hours.

Blitzkrieg.
